Thesis Overview

Presented at the 9th International Conference on Water and Flood Management (ICWFM 2023), Dhaka, Bangladesh (ISBN: 978-984-35-4948-8), this study analyzes the spatiotemporal variations of meteorological drought in Bangladesh using the Standardized Precipitation Index (SPI). Rainfall data from 23 meteorological stations (1966–2022) were analyzed with R Studio for SPI calculations, and spatial drought mapping was conducted using the Inverse Distance Weighted (IDW) method in ArcGIS. This approach highlights drought patterns and their spatial distribution across the country.
